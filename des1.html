Introduction:
Haritha Kanda is an excellent destination for beginners looking to experience hiking and camping. This guide provides essential information to help you plan your adventure.
Route:
Start from Colombo and proceed to Avissawella > Kithulgala > Ginigathhena > Bogawanthalawa > Bogawana Estate. Upon arrival at Bagowanthalawa, local three-wheel drivers can guide you to the starting point of the hike.
Transportation:
Options include bikes, three-wheelers, and public transport.
Weather:
The weather can be windy and misty. Mornings are particularly cold, so ensure you have warm clothing.

The hike is approximately 45 minutes long along a clear path. Local residents can provide directions if needed.
