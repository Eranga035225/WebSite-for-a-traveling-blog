Introduction:
Wangedigala is a one of famous hikers destination in srilanka which is located between badulla colombo route near kalupahana area The trek to Wangedigala offers adventurers a diverse landscape, leading them through pine forests, verdant grasslands, and cascading waterfalls, culminating in a breathtaking panoramic vista from the summit. Esteemed by locals, this hike demands considerable stamina, typically spanning 3 to 4 hours for the average hiker. Moreover, Wangedigala's cool climate and awe-inspiring views make it a sought-after camping spot, enticing outdoor enthusiasts to bask in its natural splendor.
Route:
Starting the trek from Kalupahana Weli Oya Bridge:
This is the easiest accessible route by public transport since you can start the trek from Weli Oya bridge which is located closer to the Colombo – Badulla Main Route and closer to Kalupahana1. Starting the trek from Bambarakanda falls:
This is the most famous route. The trail head is located approximately 1km towards Kalupahana from Bambarakanda. If you are traveling from Bambaraknda, after 750m towards Kalupahana, you have to take a sharp turn to your right hand side. Then you will see a staircase on your right hand side. That’s where you have to start descending until you find the trail head1. From Belihuloya, you need to travel approximately 14km along the Colombo – Batticaloa Highway & then about 3.2km along the Kalupahana – Ohiya Road to reach Wangedigala
